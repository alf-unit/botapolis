## Notes on sourcing and caveats

- **First-party sources** used wherever possible: Shopify App Store listings; official integration directories at klaviyo.com/integrations, gorgias.com/apps, yotpo.com/integrations, smile.io/integrations, loyaltylion.com/integrations, loox.app/integrations, loopreturns.com/integrations, help.skio.com, help.stay.ai, help.rebuyengine.com, support.aftership.com, signifyd.com, polaranalytics.com/integrations, limespot.com/integrations, inventory-planner.com/integrations, prediko.io, cogsy.com/integrations, trypencil.com, pebblely.com, and adcreative.com.
- **`integrates_with` column** is restricted to integrations between the 34 listed tools that are documented in first-party sources. Where directories were not exhaustively published (e.g., Signifyd, Shopify Sidekick, Pebblely, AdCreative.ai, Flair AI, Booth AI, Inventory Planner, Prediko, Cogsy, Pencil), it is marked **НЕ НАЙДЕНО** rather than guessed — these tools may still integrate with list members in practice (e.g., via Shopify webhooks, Zapier, or non-public partnerships), but no verifiable documentation was located.
- **Klaviyo's** in-app integration directory is only fully visible to logged-in Klaviyo accounts, so the `integrates_with` set for Klaviyo reflects partnerships documented by the partner tools themselves rather than a single Klaviyo-published list.
- **Yotpo** is a multi-product brand (Reviews, Loyalty & Referrals, SMSBump/SMS & Email, Subscriptions); the integrations listed cover the Yotpo suite as a whole.
- **Loop Subscriptions** vs **Loop Returns** are two separate companies (loopwork.co vs loopreturns.com) — both retained as distinct rows.
- **Skio** was acquired by Recharge in 2024 (now branded "Skio, a Recharge Company"); they remain distinct Shopify App Store products with distinct integrations.
- **Booth AI** appears to have ceased its product-photography service in 2024 per third-party reporting; data point flagged accordingly. The brand and any prior Shopify integration should not be used in active comparison content without re-verification.
- **Pencil's** Shopify integration is via partnership/API rather than a Shopify App Store installable app; programmatic SEO pages should describe the relationship as a "partnership/API integration," not a native app install.
- **Shopify Sidekick** is not a third-party app but Shopify's own native AI agent inside the Admin. The `integrates_with` field is marked НЕ НАЙДЕНО because Sidekick's third-party integration surface is an extension framework (Nosto, for example, has announced support) rather than direct partnerships with the other 33 tools at the time of research.
- Where vendor marketing claims (e.g., "deep integration," "best-in-class") appeared, they were translated into concrete attributes the listing supports (App Store presence, Built-for-Shopify badge, native checkout extension, Shopify Plus partner status) rather than passed through unchanged.
