Shopify Sidekick is the rare entry on a pricing site with no pricing decision to make: it's bundled free into every Shopify plan, with no separate charge. So the honest version of "how much does Sidekick cost" is "the price of your Shopify subscription, and not a cent more." The real question isn't cost — it's what Sidekick replaces, and where you still need a dedicated tool.

This page covers the plan structure Sidekick rides on, what it can and can't do, and where it doesn't displace specialist tools. Verified 2026-06-04; confirm current Shopify plan pricing at shopify.com/pricing.

## Bottom line up front

- **Best for:** every Shopify merchant, especially small-to-mid stores without dedicated copywriters, designers, or analysts.
- **Avoid if:** you're not on Shopify, you need a customer-facing chatbot, or you need AI tied to external knowledge bases.
- **Standout strength:** native, context-aware AI that takes action in your admin — at zero marginal cost.
- **Biggest weakness:** merchant-facing only, with limited third-party app data visibility — it's a copilot, not a replacement for specialist tools.

## The "pricing" is your Shopify plan (verified 2026-06-04)

| Shopify plan | Monthly | Sidekick included? |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | $39/mo | Yes |
| Shopify | $105/mo | Yes |
| Advanced | $399/mo | Yes |
| Plus | from ~$2,300/mo | Yes |

There is **no Sidekick-specific charge, tier, or usage fee**. The capability is the same across plans — what differs between Shopify tiers (transaction rates, staff accounts, report depth) is unrelated to Sidekick. If you have an active Shopify subscription, you have Sidekick. Confirm the current plan prices on Shopify's own pricing page, as they vary by region and contract term (Plus, for example, runs roughly $2,300/mo on a 3-year term versus $2,500/mo on a 1-year).

## What you actually get for $0 extra

Sidekick is a context-aware AI grounded on your store data, and the differentiator versus a generic chatbot is that it can take action:

- **Take-action agent** — creates discounts, edits products, builds collections, and changes theme settings from natural language.
- **AI image generation** — up to 5 banner or product images per prompt, saved to Files.
- **Theme section generator** — builds custom theme sections from plain English.
- **Flow automation builder** — describe an automation and Sidekick builds the Shopify Flow workflow.
- **Custom code generation** — generates small apps and code to extend store functionality.
- **Sidekick Pulse** — proactively analyzes store data and surfaces recommendations.
- **Multimodal** — text, voice, or Apple Watch, respecting each staff member's admin permissions.

For a non-technical merchant, this raises the floor on what you can do in the admin without hiring out — and it costs nothing on top of the plan you're already paying for.

## What Sidekick is — and what it isn't

The pricing is free, so the only way to overpay is to misunderstand the scope and skip a tool you actually need. Operator and reviewer takes are clear on the boundaries:

> Sidekick handles straightforward configuration tasks well. It saves time navigating through menus.
>
> — AdsX 2026 third-party review

> It will not design a beautiful, award-winning homepage banner—it will just place the elements where you tell it to.
>
> — Create8 review

Two boundaries matter for your stack decision:

1. **Merchant-facing only.** Sidekick helps *you* run the store; it does not talk to your customers. It is not a support chatbot — if you need customer-facing chat, that's a separate category ([Gorgias](/tools/gorgias), [Tidio](/tools/tidio)), and Sidekick doesn't replace it.
2. **Limited third-party visibility.** Sidekick sees your Shopify data well, but it has limited visibility into third-party app data unless that app uses the Sidekick app-extension framework. It won't replace a real CDP or analytics platform.

## When it's worth using — and when you still need more

**Use it (there's no cost reason not to) when:**
- You're a small-to-mid store without dedicated copywriters, designers, or analysts.
- You spend time on admin navigation, basic content drafting, theme tweaks, or building Flow automations.
- You want a productivity floor raised at zero marginal cost.

**You still need a dedicated tool when:**
- You need customer-facing chat or support automation — that's Gorgias or Tidio, not Sidekick.
- You need real multi-channel analytics or a CDP — Sidekick's third-party visibility is limited.
- You need strategic creative or art direction — Sidekick places elements, it doesn't design.

## Alternatives — or rather, complements

Because Sidekick is free and merchant-facing, the tools people compare it against are usually solving a different problem:

- **[Gorgias](/tools/gorgias)** / **[Tidio](/tools/tidio)** — customer-facing support and chat. Sidekick doesn't replace these; it complements them on the merchant side.
