[Stay AI](/tools/stay-ai) is the subscription platform built around AI-driven retention. This guide covers what its AI does and the scale at which it pays off.

## AI retention, natively on Shopify

Stay AI is a native Shopify subscription platform (built on Shopify's checkout), but its differentiator is not billing — it is retention intelligence. The core engines:

- **RetentionEngine** — AI ranks save-offers per subscriber, personalizing cancel flows with pauses, discounts, and surprise gifts to save churners.
- **WinbackEngine** — automated reactivation triggered by churn-risk signals.
- **Predictive churn alerts** — flags high-risk subscribers before they cancel.
- **ExperienceEngine** — personalizes the subscriber experience (dynamic carousels, in-portal banners, A/B tests) with reinforcement-learning models that self-optimize over time.

The proof point is Stay AI's own published OLIPOP case study: a 26% reduction in active churn and a 35% lift in subscription revenue after migrating off Recharge.

## The honest caveat: a high floor

Stay AI publishes a **single $499/mo plan + a 1% transaction fee + $0.19 per subscription order**, with custom pricing above. There is no multi-tier entry for smaller brands, so below a few thousand active subscribers the AI machinery does not pay back the floor. The ecosystem is also smaller than Recharge's. See the [Stay AI pricing breakdown](/pricing/stay-ai).

## Who this is for

Stay AI fits Shopify and Shopify Plus DTC brands with enough subscription volume that a few points of retained churn clears the $499/mo floor several times over — and that want AI running their save-and-winback flows.
