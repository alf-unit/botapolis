[Attentive](/tools/attentive) is the enterprise SMS leader for ecommerce, with one of the deepest native Shopify integrations in the category. This guide covers its features and, just as importantly, who it is and is not for.

## Native Shopify integration

Attentive is a Shopify Plus Technology Partner with deep native integration: checkout opt-in extensions, native purchase and order-data sync, and identity resolution that ties anonymous browsers to known subscribers. The integration is genuinely deep — the question is rarely whether Attentive connects well, but whether your store is at the scale its pricing assumes.

## The features that matter

- **Two-tap patented signup** — the opt-in flow that drives Attentive's strong list growth, a real differentiator at acquisition.
- **AI Journeys** and the broader AI suite (AI Pro predictive analytics, AI Grow signup optimization) for automated, personalized SMS sequences.
- **Multi-channel** — SMS, email, push, and RCS messaging from one platform.
- **Attentive Signal** — identity resolution that improves targeting.
- **White-glove service** — managed customer success that many advanced features route through.

## The honest caveat: enterprise pricing

Attentive has no public pricing tiers. Pricing is hidden behind a demo, contracts typically run 6–12 months, and there is usually a $2K–$3K quarterly minimum spend. Two more honest notes: revenue-attribution transparency is limited, and many advanced features are locked behind the White Glove team rather than self-serve. This is a platform built for mid-market and enterprise brands ready to make SMS a primary revenue channel — not a tool to test on a small list.

## Who this is for

Attentive fits mid-market and enterprise ecommerce brands, especially in the US/UK/AU/CA, that will commit to SMS as a core channel and can absorb the minimums. For a smaller Shopify store that wants SMS without enterprise contracts, [Postscript](/tools/postscript) is the more honest entry — see the [best SMS roundup](/best/best-ai-sms-tool-shopify) and [Attentive vs Klaviyo SMS](/compare/attentive-vs-klaviyo) for the alternatives.
