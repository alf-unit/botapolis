Loox sells visual proof: photo and video reviews that look like part of your store rather than a bolted-on widget, and the polish is genuinely the best in the category. The pricing question isn't whether the widgets are good — they are. It's that the features most brands sign up for, video reviews and the AI suite, sit at the $49.99 Convert tier, and billing scales with your order volume rather than a flat seat price.

This page does the real math: every tier, what unlocks where, how the order-volume pricing changes your bill, and where Judge.me or Yotpo is the better call. Prices verified 2026-06-04 from loox.app/pricing.

## Bottom line up front

- **Best for:** Shopify and Shopify Plus DTC brands where visual proof drives conversion — apparel, beauty, home, gifts.
- **Avoid if:** you need an unlimited free plan (Judge.me), sell off-Shopify (Loox is Shopify-only), or want loyalty and SMS in the same platform.
- **Standout strength:** the best-looking photo and video review widgets in the category, with set-and-forget automated request emails.
- **Biggest weakness:** the conversion features (video, AI, referrals) are all gated to the $49.99 Convert tier — Beginner is a genuine starter.

## The tiers in numbers (verified 2026-06-04)

| Plan | Monthly | Order limit | What unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Beginner | $14/mo | 100 monthly orders | Photo reviews, automated request emails, photo-review discounts, multilingual |
| Convert | $49.99/mo | Unlimited | + video reviews, Google Shopping syndication, referrals, AI suite, remove Loox branding |
| Unlimited | $299.99/mo | Unlimited | + unlimited review requests, unlimited referrals, unlimited upsells, priority support |

All plans bill through Shopify's subscription system with no annual commitment and a free trial. Two structural facts: there's **no free plan** (only a trial), and billing tracks **order volume**, not subscribers — Beginner caps at 100 monthly orders, the paid tiers above remove the cap.

## The Beginner-to-Convert step is the real decision

Beginner at $14/mo looks cheap, and for a small store collecting text-and-photo reviews at low order volume it is. But it's missing the features most brands actually want, and it caps at 100 monthly orders. The moment you need video reviews — the highest-converting review format — or the AI suite, you're on Convert at $49.99/mo.

What Convert ($49.99/mo) adds:

- **Video reviews** — the format that lifts conversion most, locked out of Beginner entirely.
- **Google Shopping and social syndication** — push reviews to Google Shopping, Meta Shops, TikTok Shop, the Shop App.
- **Referrals program** — reward-tracked referrals with friend discount codes.
- **The AI suite** — smart visual sorting (best reviews first), review highlights, AI review stories, auto-translation, and AI reply assistance.
- **Branding removal** — drop the "powered by Loox" mark.

For most DTC brands serious about reviews, Convert is the product and Beginner is the trial-with-a-price. Cost it as $49.99/mo, not $14.

## How order-volume pricing changes your bill

Loox bills on monthly order count, not subscriber count, and this matters more than the headline price. Two stores collecting a similar number of reviews can pay very differently:

- A **high-AOV, low-order-count** brand (say, furniture or premium goods) stays cheap — fewer orders, lower tier.
- A **low-AOV, high-order-count** brand (say, accessories or consumables) hits the order ceiling faster and pays more for the same widget surface.

Beginner's 100-monthly-order cap is the first wall; Convert and Unlimited remove it. Model your order volume, not your revenue, when picking a tier — that's the dimension the bill tracks.

**It pays back when:**
- You're a visual-first DTC brand where photo and video proof lifts conversion (apparel, beauty, home, gifts).
- Widget design matters — you want reviews that look native to the store.

**It doesn't pay back when:**
