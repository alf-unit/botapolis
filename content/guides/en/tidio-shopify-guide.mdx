[Tidio](/tools/tidio) is the live chat app most Shopify stores should run, and its Lyro AI agent is the feature that earns it a place in 2026. This guide covers what it does and where its limits are.

## Live chat, built for Shopify

Tidio installs from the Shopify App Store and surfaces order data, cart recovery prompts, and product cards directly inside the chat conversation — so an agent (or the AI) answers "where's my order" with the order in front of them. Setup takes under 30 minutes, which is part of why it is the default pick for SMB and mid-market stores.

## The Lyro AI agent

The standout feature is **Lyro**, Tidio's AI support agent. Per Tidio's own press release, Lyro independently handles 64% of customer requests on average (peaking at 90%) — routine questions answered without a human, on your storefront. For a small team, that deflection is the difference between drowning in tickets and keeping up.

## The honest caveats

Two limits worth knowing before you build around it. First, **you cannot run Lyro AI and custom flows in the same conversation** — it is one or the other per chat, so you choose between scripted flows and AI handling for a given path. Second, flow analytics are limited, and pricing scales quickly once chat volume rises (Free $0 for 50 conversations, Starter $29/mo, Growth from $59/mo). See the [Tidio pricing breakdown](/pricing/tidio).

## Who this is for

Tidio fits SMB and mid-market Shopify stores that want affordable live chat plus real AI deflection. It leads our [best live chat roundup](/best/best-live-chat-app-shopify).
