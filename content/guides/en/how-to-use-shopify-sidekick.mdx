[Shopify Sidekick](/tools/shopify-sidekick) is the AI assistant built into your Shopify admin — free with every plan, no install. This guide covers what it can do, how to prompt it well, and where it stops.

## What Sidekick can do

Sidekick is a conversational AI in your admin that handles open-ended questions and multi-step tasks in plain language. The capabilities worth knowing in 2026:

- **Content and image generation** — blog posts, product descriptions, and on-demand images (it generates up to 5 AI images from a prompt).
- **Shopify Flow building** — describe an automation and Sidekick writes the Flow; nearly 50% of Q1 2026 Flows were built with it.
- **Custom app building** — describe an app and Sidekick builds it (plan-gated to Grow, Advanced, and Plus as of April 2026).
- **Data analysis and store management** — analyze data, manage orders, edit products by asking.
- **Saved skills** — save up to 25 reusable prompts, run them with `/`, share by URL.
- **Sidekick Pulse** — works proactively in the background, surfacing personalized recommendations from your store data.

## How to write prompts that work

The single biggest lever is **framing prompts as tasks, not questions** (see step 2 above). Give Sidekick a concrete action, a time window, and a metric, and you get an executable result instead of generic advice. Specificity is the difference between "here's some advice on discounts" and a discount that's built and ready for you to confirm.

## The honest limits

Two things Sidekick does *not* do. It operates on the **merchant side only** — it cannot answer customer questions, process returns, or provide live chat, so it is not a support tool ([Gorgias](/tools/gorgias) or [Tidio](/tools/tidio) is). And it **requires confirmation before executing changes**, which is a safety feature, not a flaw — always verify before confirming.

## Who this is for

Sidekick is the free, native AI baseline every Shopify merchant should use first, especially small-to-mid stores without dedicated copywriters or analysts.
