[Omnisend](/tools/omnisend) is the ecommerce-focused email and SMS platform built around Shopify stores. This guide covers the integration, what you get, and when it is the right pick.

## How the Shopify integration works

Omnisend is Built-for-Shopify with a one-click install from the Shopify App Store. It syncs your store data natively and supports Shopify Flows, so setup is genuinely fast — most stores are sending within an hour. The integration depth is the reason Omnisend feels purpose-built for Shopify rather than adapted to it.

## What you get

Omnisend combines email, SMS, and push in a single tool — you are not stitching together two vendors for cross-channel automation. The standout for smaller stores is that **ecommerce automation is included in the free tier**, where competitors gate it behind paid plans. Support is a real strength too: 24/7 live chat with roughly a 4-minute average response, which compounds for time-constrained operators. Pricing scales predictably (Free for 250 contacts, Standard from $16/mo at 500 contacts, Pro from $59/mo).

## The honest caveat

Omnisend trades some depth for that simplicity. Reviewers flag missing advanced features (deeper flow audits, granular analytics), pop-up builder limitations, and sales attribution that lags behind Klaviyo's. If you are running sophisticated, predictive segmentation at scale, Klaviyo's engine is deeper — see the [Omnisend pricing breakdown](/pricing/omnisend).

## When Omnisend is the right call

Omnisend is the cleaner pick for Shopify stores under roughly 2,000 subscribers that want ecommerce automation, cross-channel messaging, and fast support without Klaviyo-level cost or complexity.
