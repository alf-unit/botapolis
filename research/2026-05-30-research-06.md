# Affiliate & Partner Programs for 34 Ecommerce / AI Tools — Verified Snapshot (May 2026)

## TL;DR
- **22 of 34 tools** run a publicly verifiable affiliate or partner program; **8 are partner/agency-only with gated commission terms**; **4 have no findable program** (Pebblely, Prediko, Cogsy, Booth AI); and **Shopify Sidekick** has no standalone program because it is a feature of Shopify, covered by the broader Shopify Affiliate/Partner programs.
- The richest **pure affiliate** offerings (open application, transparent commission, public link) are **Omnisend, Tidio, ManyChat, Yotpo, Loox, Postscript, Gorgias, AdCreative.ai, Triple Whale, AfterShip, Mailchimp (MonkeyRewards), and Flair AI** — most pay 10–50% recurring for 6–24 months.
- The most opaque/gated programs are **Klaviyo, Attentive, Signifyd, Northbeam, Polar Analytics, Stay AI, Skio, Inventory Planner, Loop Subscriptions, Loop Returns, and Smile.io** — all require an application and publish little or no commission detail publicly.

## Key Findings

The 34 tools fall into four buckets:

1. **Open affiliate programs with public terms (12 tools):** Omnisend, Tidio, ManyChat, Yotpo, Loox, Postscript, Gorgias, AdCreative.ai, Triple Whale, AfterShip, Mailchimp, Flair AI. These publish a commission percentage, cookie window or attribution period, and run on a recognized affiliate platform (Impact, PartnerStack, Rewardful, or a direct Tune/HasOffers stack).
2. **Agency/partner programs (some with disclosed %, most gated) (16 tools):** Klaviyo, Attentive, LoyaltyLion, Recharge, Stay AI, Skio, Loop Subscriptions, Loop Returns, Rebuy, Signifyd, Northbeam, Polar Analytics, Smile.io, Inventory Planner, LimeSpot, Pencil. A program exists, but commission % is either undisclosed or stated only as "tiered revenue share."
3. **Integration/tech-partner only — no commission (1 tool):** Judge.me explicitly does not run an affiliate program (confirmed publicly by its founder in the company's feedback portal).
4. **No program found or n/a (5 tools):** Pebblely, Prediko, Cogsy, Booth AI (likely defunct), Shopify Sidekick (Shopify feature, rolled into Shopify's broader Affiliate/Partner programs).

## Recommendations

**For an agency or content creator building a recurring SaaS affiliate stack today:**

1. **Apply first to the high-payout transparent programs** — ManyChat (up to 50% recurring, 120-day cookie), Tidio (30% lifetime), Triple Whale Affiliate (up to 20% for 6 months), AdCreative.ai (30–40% recurring, 90-day cookie), Yotpo (30% then competitive rate, 90-day cookie), Postscript (10% for 12 months), Gorgias (20% for 2 years). These have the lowest friction and the most predictable income.
2. **Treat agency/partner programs as relationship plays, not commission plays.** Klaviyo, Attentive, Recharge, Stay AI, Skio, Rebuy, LoyaltyLion, Northbeam, Polar Analytics, Loop Returns, Signifyd, and Inventory Planner all gate commission or require services. Apply only if you can deliver implementation services — they're designed to compensate billable agency hours plus rev share, not pure referrals. Recharge in particular publishes a clear 10% × 12-month figure for agency referrals — solid but only realized if you can act as an integration agency.
3. **Skip the four "not found" tools (Pebblely, Prediko, Cogsy, Booth AI) until verified directly.** Do not link affiliate URLs you cannot confirm — Booth AI in particular appears to be in an uncertain operational state (Crunchbase lists it as "permanently closed").
4. **For Mailchimp, choose the right track:** MonkeyRewards only pays bill credits (not cash) and is for existing customers; Mailchimp & Co pays real cash commission (25% new / 5% managed) but requires agency-level qualification and Payoneer payout.
5. **Re-verify quarterly.** Recharge moved off PartnerStack to its in-house Lasso platform on 15 March 2026 — the Recharge Agency Partner FAQs page states verbatim: "As of March 15th, 2026, we no longer use PartnerStack." This kind of switch is common and breaks previously published links. Set a 90-day cadence to re-check program URLs and terms.

**Threshold that should change these recommendations:** if you find a tool's commission % on its public page contradicts what's in this CSV (especially Klaviyo, Yotpo Reviews, Recharge, or LoyaltyLion, which periodically restructure tiers), trust the first-party page over this report.

## Caveats

- **Cookie windows are the weakest data point in this CSV.** Most tools simply do not publish them. Where a cookie window appears (e.g., Omnisend 60 days, ManyChat 120 days, Yotpo 90 days, Recharge 90 days, Tidio 30 days, AdCreative.ai 90 days, Flair AI 60-day Rewardful default), it is either on the first-party page or repeatedly confirmed across third-party listings. Otherwise "Not stated."
- **Klaviyo's "20% recurring / 90-day cookie"** is widely repeated by third-party directories (affiliateinfohub.com, sender.net) but **does not appear on Klaviyo's official Partner Program page or in the Partner Terms PDF.** Klaviyo officially calls it a tiered (BASIC→ELITE) revenue share with quarterly payouts. The 20% figure should be treated as unconfirmed.
- **Recharge** publishes a clear "10% revenue share for 12 months on qualified merchant referrals, paid monthly via Lasso" in its Agency Partner Program support article, along with a 90-day attribution window. The Ambassador program (for non-agency individuals) has tiered commissions based on the merchant's product plan.
- **AdCreative.ai's 30% recurring (up to 40% tiered) and 90-day cookie** are both verifiable: 30% recurring and the tiered up-to-40% structure are on the first-party affiliate page; the 90-day cookie is corroborated by Post Affiliate Pro's directory ("The cookie duration is 90 days, allowing ample time for conversions to be tracked").
- **Flair AI's** affiliate program runs on Rewardful's subdomain (flair-ai-1.getrewardful.com); the 60-day cookie is Rewardful's default per Rewardful's own docs ("The default cookie length is 60 days"). Flair has not publicly customized this, so it is the most likely effective window — but not confirmed on the Flair page itself.
- **Yotpo's affiliate program is for the Reviews product only** — Loyalty/SMS referrals run through the separate Yotpo Partner Program with different (gated) terms.
- **Triple Whale runs two programs in parallel:** the Affiliate Program (10–20% for 6 months, tiered) and the Agency Partner Program (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum on PartnerStack with revenue share tied to MRR — partners must reach $1,000+ MRR referred to qualify, per the Triple Whale Help Center).
- **LimeSpot** is the one tool where I found a commission range (10–20%) only in third-party affiliate directories with no first-party affiliate page locatable. Treat as unconfirmed.
- **Booth AI** is listed as "permanently closed" on Crunchbase but its LinkedIn shows 2024 activity. No affiliate program found regardless of operational status.
- **Pencil's** Tapfiliate signup link in the website footer redirects to Tapfiliate's marketing page rather than a public Pencil-specific commission page, strongly suggesting application-gated terms. Pencil itself appears to be operating (Pencil Pro product still offered) — no AppLovin shutdown confirmed.
- **Several "partner programs" listed here (Signifyd, Stay AI, Skio, Northbeam, Polar Analytics, Attentive)** are agency/tech partnerships, not pure affiliate. They will not pay a content creator for a click-through; they pay agencies and tech vendors with services and rev share.
- All commission rates are subject to change. Affiliate program economics in this category have moved meaningfully in the last 12 months (Yotpo launched its Reviews-only affiliate program; Triple Whale restructured tiers; Recharge migrated platforms). Re-verify before publishing affiliate disclosures.
