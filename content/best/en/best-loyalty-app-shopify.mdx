## Two loyalty tools, two clearly different buyers

Loyalty on Shopify in 2026 has two serious tools in our catalogue, and they sort by store stage rather than by who pays us. [Smile.io](/tools/smile-io) (8.6/10) is the easy, fast, points-and-VIP program most stores should start with — and it has a free tier. [LoyaltyLion](/tools/loyaltylion) (8.5/10) is the customized, on-brand program for mid-market and Shopify Plus brands that have outgrown a templated widget. We are not padding the list past these two.

One disclosure: of the two, only **Smile.io** runs an affiliate program we earn from. It also happens to be the right first pick for most stores, and we tell you below exactly where LoyaltyLion (which pays us nothing) wins instead. Prices verified 2026-06-04 (LoyaltyLion 2026-05-30).

## #1 — Smile.io (the easy pick, with a real free tier)

[Smile.io](/tools/smile-io) is the loyalty app most Shopify stores should install first. It is the easiest program to launch — points, VIP tiers, and referrals live in days, not weeks — and its Klaviyo integration is a genuine strength for tying loyalty into retention emails. The support team is praised by name across reviews. For a store that wants a branded loyalty program working quickly without a specialist, it is the clear starting point.

Pricing is freemium and worth understanding before you commit: Free $0 (under 200 monthly orders), Standard $79/mo (1,000 orders, 2 integrations), Growth $199/mo (2,500 orders, VIP tiers, Loyalty Hub, unlimited integrations), and Plus $999/mo on annual billing only. The honest caveat: the features that make loyalty powerful — VIP tiers and API access — are gated behind the $199 Growth and $999 Plus plans, and customization on the lower tiers is limited. For a small store, the free or Standard tier is real value; for one that needs VIP mechanics, budget for Growth. See the [Smile.io pricing breakdown](/pricing/smile-io) or [LoyaltyLion vs Smile.io](/compare/loyaltylion-vs-smile-io).

## #2 — LoyaltyLion (the customized mid-market pick)

[LoyaltyLion](/tools/loyaltylion) (8.5/10) is the choice when a templated points widget is not enough — when you want a loyalty program that looks and behaves like part of your brand, with customizable points, tiers, and referrals plus advanced analytics. Its Shopify and Klaviyo integrations are deep (it holds Klaviyo Elite status), and the customer-success team is a repeated strength. For mid-market and Shopify Plus DTC brands treating loyalty as a real retention channel, that depth is the differentiator.

Pricing starts higher and has no free production tier: Classic from $199/mo (roughly 500–800 monthly orders), with Advanced and Plus on custom pricing for higher volume; there is a feature-limited free Shopify-app tier to trial, but the working plans begin at $199/mo. Honest caveats: pricing escalates with order volume, multi-store and multi-currency support is limited, and some merchants report slow response on critical issues. LoyaltyLion earns its slot specifically when customization and analytics depth justify the floor — below that, Smile.io is the cleaner call. The [LoyaltyLion pricing breakdown](/pricing/loyaltylion) covers the bands.

## The honest verdict

Start with **Smile.io** — for most Shopify stores, the easiest setup plus a real free tier under 200 monthly orders is the honest answer, and it scales to VIP mechanics on Growth when you need them. Move to **LoyaltyLion** when you have outgrown a templated program and need a customized, on-brand loyalty experience with deeper analytics, accepting the $199/mo floor and no free production plan.

We do not list a third loyalty app, because the catalogue does not have a third we would put in front of an operator in mid-2026. If your retention stack also needs reviews and UGC under one vendor, [Yotpo](/tools/yotpo) bundles loyalty with reviews — a heavier, enterprise-priced stack covered in our reviews roundup, and a different buying decision.
