[Loop Returns](/tools/loop-returns) is the returns platform built to retain revenue rather than just process refunds. This guide covers its AI flows, its Shopify integration, and where it fits.

## Native Shopify integration

Loop Returns is Built-for-Shopify with a deep native integration into Shopify checkout and customer accounts. That depth is what lets it run **exchange-first flows** inside your own store experience — when a customer starts a return, Loop's AI-recommended workflows guide them toward an exchange or store credit before a cash refund, keeping the revenue in the business. The self-serve customer portal handles the whole flow without a support ticket.

## What the AI does

The AI layer surfaces smart exchange recommendations and routes returns down the path most likely to retain revenue — a size swap, a different product, or store credit rather than a refund. Combined with detailed customization and integrations, it turns the returns experience from a pure cost center into a retention surface.

## The honest caveats

Two things to weigh. **Pricing is not competitive at the lowest tiers** — Checkout+ is free only at 10,000+ annual orders, and the paid floor is Essential at $155/mo (Advanced $272/mo annual). For a smaller store, that floor is steep relative to return volume. And **edge cases still require manual support intervention** — the automation handles the common paths well but you will not eliminate hands-on returns entirely. See the [Loop Returns pricing breakdown](/pricing/loop-returns).

## Who this is for

Loop Returns fits Shopify and Shopify Plus apparel, footwear, and lifestyle brands ($1M–$50M+ revenue) with meaningful return volume, where exchange-first retention clears the cost.
