## Why this listing is short — and why we are not padding it

Most "Best AI support tool for Shopify" round-ups you can find in 2026 list five to seven options. Many of them include generic helpdesks (Zendesk, Intercom, Help Scout) that are not Shopify-native, or rebadged chat tools that do not actually do helpdesk work. We do not catalogue those tools at Botapolis — they are documented in our [Blueprint](/about) as out of scope for the Shopify-AI niche. Padding this listicle with non-Shopify-native helpdesks to look fuller would be dishonest to a reader who searched for "best AI support tool for Shopify" specifically.

The honest 2026 picture: in our catalogue, there is **one dedicated AI-helpdesk specialist purpose-built for Shopify** — Gorgias — and several adjacent-category tools that solve overlapping but different jobs. We list Gorgias as the recommendation and below that explain the adjacent-category options for operators whose actual job is not what Gorgias does best. A reader who needs only chat-led pre-purchase conversion has different needs than a reader handling 2,000 post-purchase tickets/month.

## #1 — Gorgias (the realistic dedicated pick)

Gorgias is one of the few certified customer-service platforms for Shopify Plus and is the only purpose-built Shopify-native AI helpdesk in our 2026 catalogue. The platform ships ticket-based pricing from $10/mo Starter (50 tickets, 3 seats) scaling cleanly to enterprise at 5,000+ tickets, with the AI Agent priced as a separate consumption add-on ($0.90/resolution annual or $1.00/resolution monthly, Shopify-only). What makes Gorgias the realistic pick is the Shopify-native depth: order edit, refund, and cancel actions live directly inside the ticket view, the OpenAI partnership ships the AI Agent grounded on Shopify orders and the merchant's KB, and the unified inbox handles email + chat + voice + SMS without leaving the helpdesk.

Two honest caveats per the 2025-2026 G2 review pattern: the AI Agent is double-billed — each AI resolution costs the per-resolution fee AND counts as a helpdesk ticket on most plans — so the autonomous-resolution math pays off only on tickets that would otherwise consume meaningful agent time. And ticket-based pricing scales aggressively past 2,000 tickets/month — operators near the Pro tier should model their ticket-volume projection against the Advanced tier's $900/mo monthly or $750/mo annual cost before committing.

The active affiliate program runs on PartnerStack. For operators where Shopify-native depth + AI-Agent autonomous resolution + voice/SMS channels matter, Gorgias is the band-appropriate choice. For other shapes of need, see below.

## When Gorgias is not the right fit

For brands whose actual job is closer to **chat-led pre-purchase conversion** (live chat is the primary purchase surface, helpdesk volume is light), [Tidio](/reviews/tidio) at $29/mo Growth is the cheaper functional pick. Tidio is a chat-platform with a helpdesk module and the Lyro AI agent (64-67% deflection range per Tidio first-party data); it is not a Shopify-Plus-certified helpdesk, but at this stage and price tier that gap is not the conversion bottleneck. See [Gorgias vs Tidio](/compare/gorgias-vs-tidio) for the head-to-head.

For brands already running **Klaviyo** for email and SMS, [Klaviyo Customer Agent](/reviews/klaviyo) is the multi-channel CRM's native helpdesk add-on. It is priced separately on a consumption basis and resolves support tickets inside the same customer-profile data model Klaviyo already runs for marketing. For operators consolidating post-purchase support inside Klaviyo to avoid a second vendor + data-model split, this is the realistic option. It is not, however, a Gorgias replacement at the operating-depth level — Klaviyo Customer Agent is a CRM-native helpdesk add-on, not a Shopify-Plus-certified support platform.

## The honest verdict

Pick **Gorgias** if you need a dedicated, Shopify-Plus-certified AI helpdesk handling 200+ tickets per month with ticket-based revenue attribution. That is the band the platform is purpose-built for and where the AI Agent consumption math actually pays back.

Pick **Tidio** if chat-led pre-purchase conversion is the actual revenue lever and helpdesk volume is still building. The $29/mo Growth tier handles real chat + helpdesk + Lyro AI deflection at a fraction of Gorgias pricing for early-stage stores. See the [Gorgias vs Tidio](/compare/gorgias-vs-tidio) page for the full segmented-by-stage verdict.

Pick **Klaviyo Customer Agent** as an extension of an existing Klaviyo deployment when consolidating support into the multi-channel CRM is worth the depth tradeoff against a Shopify-native helpdesk specialist.

We are not listing a fifth or sixth option. The honest answer to "best AI support tool for Shopify" in 2026 is the three above, ranked by job-to-be-done. Padding the list with non-Shopify-native generic helpdesks would not serve the reader who searched for this query.
