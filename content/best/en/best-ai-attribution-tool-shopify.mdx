## Why this list looks the way it does

Three attribution platforms cover the realistic Shopify DTC market in 2026: Northbeam, Polar Analytics, Triple Whale. We did not pad this shortlist with generic web analytics tools (GA4, Plausible) or rebadged spreadsheet-style dashboards — those solve a different job. Pricing on every line below was verified directly on each vendor's pricing page on May 30, 2026.

The honest framing matters here because **none of these three runs an active affiliate program at the time of verification**. Northbeam is `affiliate_partner: direct` with no public URL; Polar Analytics does not have a public program; Triple Whale has a PartnerStack listing but no active `affiliate_url` is published. That means every link below leads to our own review pages or vendor documentation, not to affiliate landing pages. We are not changing the ranking to surface a partner — there is no partner in this category in our 2026 catalogue.

## #1 — Northbeam (winner for $1M+ annual paid-media spend)

Northbeam publishes a $1,500/mo Starter on its own pricing page (verified 2026-05-30, with the page text "Lower than $1.5m a year in media spend") and targets mid-market through enterprise DTC brands. The methodological depth — MTA + MMM+ (high-frequency MMM retraining weekly, including offline channels like podcast / CTV / direct mail) + Apex signals-back to ad platforms — is what justifies the entry-price floor over Polar Analytics or Triple Whale. Northbeam's own August 2024 case study reports an average 34% conversion-rate improvement on Meta across 25 advertisers spending $1.5M+ via the Apex feedback model.

The G2 footprint is small (16 reviews, 4.5/5) — limited third-party validation. For brands evaluating Northbeam, that thinness is itself a signal: this is a methodology-led pick for buyers who can defend it on math, not on community consensus. Pricing above Starter is custom-quoted; MMM+ is Enterprise-only.

## #2 — Polar Analytics (winner for sub-$1M ARR warehouse-style)

Polar Analytics at $300/mo Starter is the pragmatic pick for Shopify DTC brands not yet at the $1M+/year paid-media spend tier where Northbeam pays back. The positioning is warehouse-style cohort + unified analytics + attribution basics — the methodologically conservative posture for brands wanting paid tooling from day one rather than riding a free tier and migrating later. The G2 footprint (~50+ reviews, 4.8/5) is positive and growing.

Polar Analytics publishes pricing more transparently across tiers (Starter $300 / mid-tier $499-$999 / Enterprise custom) than Northbeam (Starter floor only published; Professional and Enterprise custom-quoted). For brands modeling 12-24 month tooling spend for budget approval, that transparency is the deciding factor over Northbeam at this stage.

## #3 — Triple Whale (Free tier + entry-tier pick, with accuracy caveat)

Triple Whale ships a Free tier with first-party Triple Pixel + first/last-click attribution + 12-month lookback + 10 users — genuinely the cheapest entry into Shopify attribution in 2026. Starter at $149/mo (3,000 monthly credits, MTA, unlimited users) opens up the Moby 2 AI agent and 8+ attribution models. The platform's breadth (Triple Pixel + MTA + Moby + Compass MMM custom add-on) is real.

The honest caveat per the 2025-2026 G2 review pattern: multiple reviews flag "the attribution system is consistently buggy and unreliable" alongside billing-cancellation complaints. For brands using the data to defend ad-spend decisions to leadership, this matters. Pilot Triple Whale Free against native Shopify reporting for the first 60 days before scaling to a paid tier or before treating Triple Pixel data as gospel. The capability is real; so is the reliability concern.

## The honest verdict

Pick **Northbeam** when paid-media spend justifies enterprise MTA + MMM tooling ($1M+/year) and you can defend the price-tier jump on methodology rather than community endorsement.

Pick **Polar Analytics** when sub-$1M ARR makes Northbeam's $1,500/mo entry wrong but you want paid warehouse-style cohort analytics from day one rather than riding a free tier.

Pick **Triple Whale** for the Free tier first-party pixel test or the $149/mo Starter when budget is constrained — but treat attribution numbers as directional, not gospel, and reconcile against Shopify's native reporting for the first 60 days before making spend decisions on top of the data. The category leader for breadth, with the reliability concerns 2025-2026 G2 reviews surface honestly.
