## Why this list looks the way it does

Three platforms cover the realistic AI-email Shopify market in 2026: Klaviyo, Omnisend, Mailchimp. We did not pad this shortlist with adjacent-category tools or non-monetised options to make the listing look fuller — the honest market has three serious choices for Shopify ecommerce email, and that is what you read here. Pricing on every line below was verified directly on each vendor's pricing page on May 30, 2026.

Two market shifts reshape the comparison vs 2024-2025 advice: Mailchimp deprecated the Classic Automation Builder in June 2025 (multi-step automations now require Standard at $20/mo), and Mailchimp cut its free tier in January 2026 to 250 contacts and 500 sends per month. Klaviyo migrated billing from emails-sent to active profiles in February 2025 — the sticky-list pricing dynamic changed. Omnisend kept a more generous free tier and ships ecommerce automation in the free plan directly, which is the structural advantage at the SMB entry.

## #1 — Klaviyo (winner for $50K+ MRR Shopify DTC)

Klaviyo is the gold standard for Shopify ecommerce email at scale and the recommended email partner for Shopify Plus since the August 2022 partnership. The platform's depth — predictive analytics, real-time CDP, 80+ pre-built ecommerce flows, deep segmentation (9.0/10 G2 features score) — pays back when the operator actually runs the segmentation engine. Below $50K/mo MRR, much of that depth is unused; above $50K, Klaviyo's ROI math justifies the premium pricing.

Two honest caveats worth surfacing upfront. First, billing shifted from emails-sent to ACTIVE PROFILES on February 18, 2025 — operators with sticky lists (long inactive-contact tails) now pay for that tail. Second, above ~$10K/mo total spend, Klaviyo One enterprise becomes mandatory and adds 20% to the total bill. If you are modeling 12-month spend that projects past that threshold, fold the surcharge into the forecast before signing.

What we will not propagate: the "20% commission / 90-day cookie" line that third-party affiliate directories repeat. It does not appear on Klaviyo's official Partner Program page or in the Partner Terms PDF. The official structure is tiered BASIC→ELITE with quarterly payouts; specific rates are application-gated.

## #2 — Omnisend (winner for sub-2k subscriber Shopify stores)

Omnisend is the cleaner pick for Shopify stores below 2,000 subscribers in 2026. The free tier covers 250 contacts and 500 emails and — critically — includes ecommerce automation, which Mailchimp now gates to Standard at $20/mo and which Klaviyo's free tier does include but with a steeper learning curve. Omnisend Pro at $16/mo (500 contacts) is genuinely cheaper than Klaviyo Email at $20/mo at the same tier.

The Botapolis-relevant differentiator: Omnisend ships best-in-category support response — roughly four-minute average response on live chat per the vendor, corroborated by 9.1/10 ease-of-use on G2 (Klaviyo 8.7, Mailchimp 8.7). For time-constrained operators handling email alongside the rest of their stack, faster support compounds. Omnisend runs an active affiliate program on the Impact platform.

## #3 — Mailchimp (winner only for Essentials-tier single-flow operators)

Mailchimp wins narrowly when two conditions hold: brand familiarity makes migration costly, AND you only need a single-step welcome automation. At that exact intersection, Mailchimp Essentials at $13/mo is genuinely cheaper than Omnisend Pro at $16/mo. The moment you want multi-step flows (welcome → cart abandonment → win-back), Mailchimp pushes you to Standard at $20/mo — and the cost advantage evaporates.

The 2026 reality: Mailchimp's free tier was cut to 250 contacts / 500 sends per month in January 2026 (down from 2,000 contacts pre-cut), Classic Automation was deprecated in June 2025, and the platform shifted positioning back toward general-purpose marketing rather than Shopify-native ecommerce email. For Shopify operators starting fresh in 2026 without existing Mailchimp investment, Klaviyo or Omnisend is the more honest recommendation.

## The honest verdict

Pick **Klaviyo** when you operate above $50K/mo MRR on Shopify or Shopify Plus and will actually run the segmentation engine, predictive analytics, and ecommerce flow library. The pricing premium pays back at that scale and the partnership integration depth has no peer.

Pick **Omnisend** when you operate below 2k subscribers and want ecommerce automation in the free or low-paid tier, faster support response, and Shopify-focused depth without Klaviyo's pricing complexity. The active affiliate program is a bonus for content sites and agencies.

Pick **Mailchimp** only when existing brand investment makes migration costly AND single-step automation covers the need at Essentials $13/mo. For any other Shopify operator starting fresh in 2026, the other two are the cleaner picks.
