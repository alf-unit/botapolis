[Loox](/tools/loox) is the visual-first reviews app for Shopify, and its integrations are built to push that visual proof everywhere it can convert. This guide covers what connects and at which tier.

## Shopify-only by design

Loox is Built-for-Shopify with a deep native integration — and it is **Shopify-only by architecture** (no WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Wix). That focus is why the integration feels native rather than ported: photo and video review widgets, the discount-for-photo incentive, and automated review-request emails all run inside Shopify's flow.

## The integrations that matter

- **Multi-channel syndication** — Loox pushes reviews to Google Shopping, Meta, TikTok Shop, and the Shop app, turning collected reviews into off-site social proof. This is gated to the Convert tier and above.
- **Klaviyo deep integration** — full Klaviyo integration with review events feeding flows, so a new review can trigger a retention email. Note this is gated to the **Unlimited plan** specifically.
- **SMS review requests** — send requests via SMS, not just email (Convert and above).
- **Loox Studio** — turn reviews into branded social posts and stories.

## The honest caveat: tier gating

The integration map has real tier walls. Video reviews, the AI suite, multi-channel syndication, and referrals live on the **Convert plan ($49.99/mo)**; the Klaviyo deep integration is **Unlimited-only ($299.99/mo)**. The entry **Beginner plan ($14/mo)** covers photo reviews and request emails but little of the syndication machinery. Loox also bills on monthly order count, not subscribers. See the [Loox pricing breakdown](/pricing/loox).
