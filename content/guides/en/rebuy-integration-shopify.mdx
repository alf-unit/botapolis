[Rebuy](/tools/rebuy) is an ecommerce personalization platform that lives in your Shopify cart and checkout. This guide covers how it integrates and where it earns its keep.

## Native Shopify integration

Rebuy is Built-for-Shopify with native cart and checkout-extension integration — its **Smart Cart** replaces or augments your cart with AI-driven cross-sell and upsell recommendations, and it extends personalization into checkout and post-purchase pages. Because it hooks into Shopify's native cart and checkout extensions rather than overlaying a generic widget, the recommendations render inside your real buying flow. Rebuy connects to 35+ other tools (your email, reviews, and subscription apps), so the personalization can draw on data beyond the cart.

## What you get

The practical output is measurable AOV lift: dynamic recommendations across cart, checkout, and PDP, plus no-code and low-code building blocks so a merchandiser can configure offers without engineering. For Shopify and Shopify Plus stores with a diverse catalogue (10+ SKUs), that cross-sell layer is the lever Rebuy is built around.

## The honest caveats

Two things to weigh. **Initial widget setup can be complex** — this is a platform with real configuration depth, not a one-click app, so budget setup time (the CSM model, widely praised, helps here). And **pricing climbs steeply**: Free (basic), Build-Your-Plan from $25/mo, Starter $99/mo, Grow $749/mo. The Grow tier is steep for small brands, so model the AOV lift against the cost. Some support tickets also lag on weekends. See the [Rebuy pricing breakdown](/pricing/rebuy).

## Who this is for

Rebuy fits Shopify and Shopify Plus stores with diverse SKUs that want to lift AOV and LTV through cart, checkout, and PDP personalization.
