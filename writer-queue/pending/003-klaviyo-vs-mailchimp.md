## Why this article (the angle)

The article should challenge the stale "Klaviyo always wins for ecommerce" answer by focusing on Shopify stores under 2,000 subscribers. The core angle from research section 1: Mailchimp's free/entry value is real in a narrow band, but Klaviyo's ecommerce depth catches up fast once the store needs multi-step automation, browse abandonment, product recommendations, or serious segmentation. Lead with the 2026 Shopify integration revival hook: Mailchimp is back in the Shopify App Store via the October 26, 2021 ShopSync acquisition, so old 2019-2021 comparisons are no longer enough.
