## Why this shortlist is short — and what counts as "live chat"

Live chat on Shopify in 2026 splits cleanly: real-time chat on your storefront (with AI to deflect the repetitive questions) versus social-DM automation that lives in Instagram and Messenger. Our catalogue has one strong pick for each, and we are not padding the list with generic chat widgets. [Tidio](/tools/tidio) (8.7/10) owns the on-site live chat job; [ManyChat](/tools/manychat) (8.3/10) owns the social-DM job. We rank them by the job most people mean when they search "live chat for Shopify" — chat on your store — which is why Tidio leads.

Both run affiliate programs, so both listings below carry a partner link. Prices verified directly on each vendor's pricing page, 2026-05-30 (Tidio re-checked 2026-06-05).

## #1 — Tidio (the on-site live chat pick)

[Tidio](/tools/tidio) is the live chat app most Shopify stores should install. It does the core job well — real-time chat on your storefront, with order data, cart recovery, and product cards surfaced inside the conversation — and setup takes under 30 minutes. The differentiator in 2026 is the Lyro AI agent: per Tidio's own press release, Lyro independently handles 64% of customer requests on average (peaking at 90%), which means the volume of repetitive "where's my order" tickets your team never has to touch.

Pricing starts genuinely free: Free $0 (50 conversations/mo, 50 one-off Lyro chats), Starter $29/mo ($24.17 billed annually) for 100 conversations with 50 Lyro conversations included, Growth from $59/mo ($49.17 annual) scaling to 2,000 conversations, and Plus from $749/mo. Two honest caveats: pricing scales quickly once chat volume rises, and you cannot run Lyro AI and custom flows simultaneously — it is one or the other per conversation. For an SMB or mid-market Shopify store that wants affordable live chat plus real AI deflection, it is the clear pick. See the [Tidio pricing breakdown](/pricing/tidio), [Tidio vs Gorgias](/compare/gorgias-vs-tidio) for the helpdesk angle, or [ManyChat vs Tidio](/compare/manychat-vs-tidio).

## #2 — ManyChat (the social-DM pick — a different job)

[ManyChat](/tools/manychat) (8.3/10) earns the second slot, but with an honest framing: it is not really a storefront live chat tool. ManyChat is built for conversational marketing in Instagram, Messenger, and WhatsApp — turning comments and DMs into automated, sales-driving conversations. As an official Meta Business Partner with a no-code visual flow builder, it is the right tool when your customer acquisition lives on social and your "chat" is a DM funnel, not a website chat bubble.

Pricing is freemium: Free $0 (25 active contacts), Pro at $29/mo (2,500 contacts, AI features, 3 channels), and Business at $69/mo (7,500 contacts, all channels). The honest caveats: cost scales aggressively as your contact list grows, support quality is inconsistent (multi-day responses reported), and WhatsApp conversation fees are not included in the base plan. If your conversations happen on your Shopify storefront, Tidio is the answer; if they happen in Instagram DMs, ManyChat is — which is exactly why it ranks second here and first in our chatbot roundup. The [ManyChat pricing breakdown](/pricing/manychat) and [ManyChat vs Tidio](/compare/manychat-vs-tidio) cover the split.

## The honest verdict

Pick **Tidio** for live chat on your Shopify storefront — affordable entry, real-time chat, and a Lyro AI agent that deflects a meaningful share of tickets is the right setup for most stores. Reach for **ManyChat** only when your conversations live in Instagram and Messenger and you need social-DM automation, not an on-site chat widget — a genuinely different job.

We do not list a third "live chat" app, because the catalogue does not have a third on-site chat tool we would recommend in mid-2026. For AI support that resolves tickets autonomously at higher ticket volume, [Gorgias](/tools/gorgias) is the helpdesk-grade alternative — covered in our support roundup, a heavier tool for a heavier job.
