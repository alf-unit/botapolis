[ManyChat](/tools/manychat) is a social-DM automation tool, not a storefront app — so its Shopify "integration" works differently than most. This guide gives you the honest picture before you build flows around it.

## What the integration actually covers

ManyChat connects to Shopify to pull your product catalog directly into chat flows, so you can show products and close sales inside an Instagram or Messenger conversation. Keyword triggers are the workhorse: set a comment trigger like "DISCOUNT" and anyone who comments it gets an automated DM sequence. As an official Meta Business Partner, ManyChat is the approved way to automate Instagram, Messenger, and WhatsApp at scale.

## Where you need middleware — the honest caveat

Here is what most guides skip: ManyChat's native Shopify connection is **light**. For deeper ecommerce automations — abandoned-cart recovery, shipping confirmations, order-status flows — you typically need **Shopify Flow plus Zapier or Make** to bridge the two systems. ManyChat is excellent at the top of the funnel (turning social engagement into conversations) but it is not a replacement for an on-store ecommerce automation tool. Plan it as your social-DM acquisition layer, not your post-purchase engine.

## Setup and pricing

Setup takes roughly 15–30 minutes depending on flow complexity. Pricing is freemium: Free (25 active contacts), Pro at $29/mo (2,500 contacts, AI, 3 channels), Business at $69/mo. Two honest notes: cost scales aggressively with your contact list, and WhatsApp conversation fees are billed on top of the base plan. See the [ManyChat pricing breakdown](/pricing/manychat).

ManyChat fits DTC brands and creators whose customers find them on Instagram and who want to automate DM-driven selling.
