# Verified Pricing Comparison: 34 Shopify AI/Ecommerce Tools

**Date checked:** May 30, 2026 (subagent verifications: May 31, 2026). All prices in USD per month unless otherwise noted. "Custom" indicates pricing only available via a sales contact. Rows were verified directly against each vendor's official pricing page where possible; third-party benchmarks are explicitly flagged in the addendum.

## TL;DR

- **The cheapest verifiable Shopify ecosystem tools** are Judge.me ($15/mo flat), Loox Beginner ($9.99/mo), Pebblely Lite ($9/mo), Pencil Core ($14/mo) and Mailchimp Essentials ($13/mo). **The most expensive base-tier published prices** are Tidio Plus ($749/mo), Stay AI ($499/mo), Skio ($499/mo annual / $599/mo monthly) and Loop Subscriptions Pro ($399/mo + 0.75% + per-order fees).
- **Pricing is hidden behind "contact sales" for 6 of the 34 tools** — Attentive, Signifyd, Northbeam, Inventory Planner, LoyaltyLion Advanced/Plus, and Yotpo Premium/Enterprise. Third-party benchmarks suggest realistic spend of $1K–$5K/mo at entry and tens of thousands annually at scale; these have been marked "custom".
- **The most expensive hidden cost across the set is auto-scaling on usage metrics** — Klaviyo's active-profile bill (which counts unsubscribed-but-not-suppressed contacts), Mailchimp's contact bloat (unsubscribed contacts count unless manually archived), Gorgias's AI Agent double-billing ($0.90–$1.00 per AI resolution PLUS the ticket itself), Tidio's three parallel quotas (conversations + Lyro AI + Flow triggers), and Smile.io/Loop Subscriptions/Stay AI/Skio's per-order transaction fees on every subscription order.

## Addendum — Tools With Unverifiable / Pricing-Hidden Plans

The following vendors do not publish complete pricing publicly. Their rows above are marked "custom" or include third-party benchmarks where official numbers are absent. Treat the benchmarks as ballpark, not commitments:

- **Attentive** — entirely demo-gated; benchmarks from third-party customer reports ($2K–$3K quarterly minimum, ~$40K median annual contract).
- **Signifyd** — pricing is a percentage of approved order value; quote-only.
- **Northbeam** — official page contains no numbers; the $999–$1,500/mo Starter benchmark is third-party.
- **Inventory Planner (Sage)** — quote-only; the $244.99/mo floor is third-party.
- **LoyaltyLion** — only Classic ($199/mo) is published; Advanced and Plus are quote-only.
- **Yotpo** — Premium and Enterprise tiers across both Reviews and Loyalty are custom; module-stacking creates an effective minimum of $278/mo for Reviews + Loyalty bundled.

- **Gorgias Enterprise** — custom; qualifies at $20M+ revenue or $40K+ contract.
- **Cogsy** — vendor's own pages give two conflicting starting prices ($49/mo vs $199/mo).
